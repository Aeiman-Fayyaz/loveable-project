About SizeAI

We're on a mission to revolutionize online shopping with AI-powered size recommendations.

Our Story

SizeAI was born from frustration with online shopping returns. Our founders, Sarah and Marcus, experienced it firsthand when none of five dress sizes fit.

With Marcus’s machine learning expertise and Sarah’s fashion insight, they built an AI solution to find the perfect fit for every shopper.

Now SizeAI analyzes millions of data points with 95% accuracy and partners with 500+ brands to make shopping easier and more sustainable.
