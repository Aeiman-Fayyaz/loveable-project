How Our AI Model Works

  1. 1. Data Collection & Training

    Anonymized sizing data from millions of shoppers builds a robust dataset across brands and body types.

  2. 2. Feature Engineering

    We analyze proportions, brand consistency, and fit preferences, weighting the most predictive features.

  3. 3. Neural Network Architecture

    A multi-layer perceptron with dropout regularization learns complex fit relationships.

  4. 4. Prediction & Confidence Scoring

    Model outputs a size recommendation with a confidence score to indicate precision.
